<h2 class="border-bottom mb-3 mt-5">Dead blogs are</h2>

Blogs are dead (almost dead). People (myself included) used to write about the stuff they learned. But with AI, I'm only learning boring AI stuff that's, IMO, not worth reading about. And even when I do learn something, it's not fun to write about, what's the point, if you (the reader) could just as easily ask AI to explain it?

So I made a fun little "game" on the main page instead. Click on the house a bunch of times. It's completely vibed, I haven't learned ANYTHING from making it, but I don't mind as much. It's more art than anything, really. Sure, I could've actually learned Three.js and GSAP doing it properly... but what's the point?
